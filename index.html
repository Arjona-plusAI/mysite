Arjona +AI Help

Quick Start

Upload image or add text. Use AI Generator for backgrounds. Adjust with sliders. Export as HD PNG.

Layers

Upload images, add text layers. Reorder with Front/Back. Duplicate or Delete. Align Left/Center/Right/Middle.

Typography

Edit text content, change font family, text color, character spacing, curve bend. Upload custom TTF/OTF fonts.

3D and FX

Stroke with custom color, Glow, 3D Depth with color, Inner Shadow, Emboss, Reflection. Each has a color picker.

Mask and Erase

Select mode to move elements. Eraser brush to remove parts. Mask crop tool. Adjustable brush size. Restore erased areas.

AI Generate

Describe your image in the prompt. Choose a style preset. Generate as Background or as a new Layer.

AI Chat

Click the AI button to chat with Arjona AI. Ask design tips, get suggestions, or give voice commands.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Ctrl+Z Undo | Ctrl+Shift+Z Redo | Ctrl+D Duplicate | Delete Remove | Arrow keys Move (Shift+Arrow = 10px) | Ctrl+S Export

Privacy Policy

All editing happens in your browser. No data is stored on servers. AI image generation uses Pollinations.ai API. AI chat uses Pollinations text API.

Terms of Use

Free for personal and commercial use. AI-generated content follows Pollinations.ai terms of service. No warranty provided.
